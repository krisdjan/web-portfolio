About Ander & Kristjan

We are passionate web developers based in Estonia, specializing in creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites and web applications. Learn more about our skills and approach below.

Kristjan – Frontend & UX Developer

Kristjan got into development from the creative side — starting with design, then figuring out how to bring ideas to life in code. What began as tweaking WordPress themes turned into building full custom interfaces, learning JavaScript frameworks, and obsessing over things like button spacing and mobile breakpoints. Kristjan thrives at the point where visual design meets code. He focuses on making apps not just usable but enjoyable. If something feels off — even by a few pixels — he’ll catch it. Beyond just making things pretty, Kristjan is big on accessibility, performance, and keeping interfaces intuitive and clean. Strengths: Frontend frameworks, UX/UI design, performance optimization, accessibility Drives: Usability, aesthetics, thoughtful interactions Vibe: Sharp eye, fast iterations, asks "why" before "how"
